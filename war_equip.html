Weapon and Armor Qualities



Armour Piercing

Weapons with this quality are particularly good at punching through armour. Attacks from Armour Piercing weapons ignore 1 Armour Point. If a target has no armour, this quality has no effect. You can gain both this bonus and that of the Sure Shot talent.

Bomb

If the throw misses, roll 1d10. On a roll of a 1, the fuse goes out. On a roll of a 10, the incendiary explodes in the wielder’s hands, dealing damage to all within the area of the blast radius. On a result of 2 - 9, roll another 1d10, and the result is the number of yards away from the target that the incendiary lands, as well of the direction. The incendiary explodes in a 3 square radius. All creatures in the area must make successful Agility Tests or catch fire. Once blazing, they lose 1d10 Wounds (with no reduction for armour or Toughness Bonus) each round.

Balanced

Weapons with this quality are designed to be used in your secondary hand, usually in concert with a rapier or foil. When wielding a Balanced weapon in your secondary hand, you don’t suffer the normal –20% penalty to your Weapon Skill.

Defensive

Weapons with this quality are designed with parrying in mind. You gain a +10% bonus on parry attempts while wielding a Defensive weapon.

Experimental

On an attack roll of 96-98, the weapon jams and cannot be fire again until a successful Trade (Gunsmith) Skill Test is made. On an attack roll of 99-00, the weapon explodes, inflicting a Damage 8 hit on the wielder and destroying itself in the process.

Fast

Weapons with this quality strike quickly because they are especially nimble or long. Enemies suffer a -10% penalty when trying to parry or dodge a Fast weapon.

Impact

Weapons with this quality hit with tremendous force. If you hit with an Impact weapon, you may roll two d10 for damage and pick the higher result.

Precise

Weapons with this quality are deadly accurate. When using a Precise weapon, the Critical Value of any Critical Hits is increased by 1. You can gain both this bonus and that of the Strike to Injure talent.

Pummelling

Weapons with this quality can knock foes senseless. You gain a +10% bonus to your Strength when using the Strike to Stun talent.

Shrapnel

Firing a Shrapnel weapon does not require a Ballistic Skill Test to hit. Simply measure out a line as long as the weapon’s maximum range and 2 yards (1 square) wide. Anyone caught in this area must make a successful Agility Test or suffer the weapon’s damage.

Slow

Weapons with this quality are heavy and awkward, making them easier to avoid. Enemies gain a +10% bonus when trying to parry or dodge a Slow weapon.

Snare

On a failed Agility Test the target is restricted to free actions or actions that enable his escape. The target may attempt a Full Action to break the bonds by making a Strength Test or wriggle free by making an Agility Test. Getting assistanse (also a Full Action) the DC improves to Routine (+10%). Attacks vs. the character gain a +20% bonus

Special

See weapon description for additional special rules.

Tiring

Weapons with this quality are fatiguing to use. Their Impact quality thus only applies during the first round of melee.

Unreliable

Weapons with this quality do not always function dependably. On an attack roll of 96-99, the weapon jams and cannot be fire again until a successful Trade (Gunsmith) Skill Test is made. On an attack roll of 00, the weapon explodes, inflicting its normal damage on the wielder and destroying itself in the process.

Light armor

If you are wearing light armour (Basic) or only leather armour (Advanced), you suffer no penalties.

Medium armor

If you are wearing medium armour (Basic) or any type of mail (Advanced), you suffer a –10% penalty to your Agility Characteristic. This penalty does not apply if the only mail you are wearing is a coif.

Heavy armor

If you are wearing heavy armour (Basic) or any type of plate (Advanced), you suffer –1 penalty to your Movement Characteristic. If you are using Encumbrance, you can ignore this penalty, as the Encumbrance system already accounts for this.
